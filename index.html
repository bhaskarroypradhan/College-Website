About

Alipurduar Government Engineering and Management College (AGEMC) is a premier institution that offers cutting-edge education in the fields of engineering and management. Established with a vision to nurture the brightest minds, AGEMC provides students with a strong academic foundation, state-of-the-art facilities, and a dynamic learning environment.

The college focuses on research, innovation, and industry collaboration, ensuring students develop skills that align with modern technological advancements. Students also engage in extracurricular activities, leadership programs, and technical workshops to enhance their overall growth.

Artificial Intelligence

The Department of Artificial Intelligence covers emerging fields such as machine learning, deep learning, and neural networks. The curriculum ensures students gain real-world experience by working on AI-based projects, making them industry-ready.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering at AGEMC focuses on power generation, circuit design, and embedded systems. Students gain practical skills through hands-on projects, preparing them for careers in energy and automation industries.

Computer Science & Engineering

The Computer Science department provides training in software development, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. Students learn programming languages such as Python, Java, and C++ to tackle complex tech challenges.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

ECE students explore semiconductor technology, telecommunications, and IoT. The department offers cutting-edge research opportunities in modern communication networks and embedded systems.
